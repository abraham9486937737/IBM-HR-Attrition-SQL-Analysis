# Data Understanding & Exploration

## Objective
To explore the IBM HR Attrition dataset and understand the distribution
of employees across key dimensions before performing data cleaning
and advanced analysis.

---

## Key Explorations
- Department and job role distribution
- Gender and marital status breakdown
- Employee age range and average
- Overall attrition count and percentage

---

## Key Observations
- Attrition is not evenly distributed across departments
- Certain job roles experience higher employee turnover
- Gender-wise attrition shows noticeable variation
- Average employee age lies in the mid-career range

---

## Conclusion
Initial exploration provides a clear understanding of employee
distribution and attrition patterns, forming a strong foundation
for deeper analytical queries.
