# Intermediate HR Attrition Analysis

## Objective
To identify key drivers of employee attrition using
multi-dimensional SQL aggregations and conditional logic.

---

## Analyses Performed
- Work-life balance vs attrition
- Job satisfaction vs attrition
- Employee tenure and attrition patterns

---

## Key Insights
- Employees with poor work-life balance show higher attrition
- Low job satisfaction strongly correlates with employee exits
- Attrition is highest during early years of employment

---

## Business Impact
These insights help HR teams focus retention strategies on
early-career employees and work-life balance improvements.
