# Basic HR Attrition Analysis

## Objective
To perform foundational HR analytics using SQL aggregate functions
and grouping to identify key attrition patterns.

---

## Key Analyses Performed
- Overall employee attrition rate
- Department-wise attrition distribution
- Salary comparison between employees who stayed vs left

---

## Key Findings
- Attrition rate provides a baseline HR KPI
- Certain departments experience higher attrition
- Employees with lower average monthly income show higher attrition

---

## Conclusion
Basic aggregation analysis highlights initial risk areas and
sets the stage for deeper intermediate and advanced analysis.
